Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 December 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Blood Pressure Tracker handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

  • We do not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

b) Health Data

  • Blood pressure readings, pulse values, notes, and timestamps entered by the user.
  • This data is stored only on your device.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Display blood pressure history
  • Calculate summaries and trends
  • Export reports on user request

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. No data is uploaded to servers or cloud services.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored locally using standard device storage mechanisms. We do not have access to your data.

5. Data Deletion

You may delete all data at any time by clearing app data or uninstalling the App.

6. Children’s Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected within the App.
